StreamClip – Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. What We Collect

StreamClip collects only the OAuth access and refresh tokens provided by TikTok after you authorize the application. No other personal information is collected.

2. How We Use It

Tokens are stored locally on your server (in a Docker volume) and are used exclusively to publish video clips to your TikTok account on your behalf.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party. Tokens are only sent to TikTok's official API endpoints (open.tiktokapis.com).

4. Data Retention

Tokens are retained until you delete them or revoke access via TikTok's account settings.

5. Your Rights

You can revoke StreamClip's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's app permissions settings.
